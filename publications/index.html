    Barbara Pascal

    I am currently a CNRS Researcher in LS2N, in Nantes, France. Before that I was a postdoctoral researcher at CRIStAL for two years, taking part in the ERC project of R. Bardenet. I received the degree of Professeur-Agrégé of Mathematics in 2016 at École Normale Supérieure de Lyon, then completed my M.S. degree in Physics from the ENS Lyon, France, in 2017, and received the Ph.D degree in Signal & Image Processing from the ENSL in 2020, for my work at the Laboratoire de Physique, under the supervision of P. Abry and N. Pustelnik.
